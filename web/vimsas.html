Why:

The Enhanced editor works great. Why switch to Vim? Your productivity will increase. You'll spend more time thinking about your code and less time editing it.

Vim benefits:

  • Syntax coloring
  • Folding to allow you to see the logical flow of your code
  • Macro actions like sequential numbering
  • Regex searching of Log and List
  • Many more items...depending on how much you want to learn

    Assumptions:

  • You are familiar with Unix-like operating systems
  • You prefer (or are willing to learn) the command line over GUI IDEs.

    My Configuration:

    My syntax highlighting file-- sas.vim

    My runsas bash shell script-- runsas.sh 

          Sample session
      $ vi foo.sas

      Press Ctrl-v to suspend

      $ sasrun foo.sas

    The job runs and produces output in two windows, positioning the cursor on any WARNINGs or ERRORs.
